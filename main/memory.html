MEMORY GAME

Memory game is a simple game to train your memory

At first the app is making the board by randomly choosing place for 6 numbers twice. It will help to display appropriate image later. You have 12 chances. A chance will be used when you peek at 2 cards.

When you use all the chances you lose. The cards become unclickable. The reset button appears to restart the game. Also you will hear an appropriate sound.

When you uncover all the cards you win. The cards become unclickable and you can see information that you won x points where x is the quanity of chances you haven't used. The reset button appears to restart the game. Also you will hear an appropiert sound.
